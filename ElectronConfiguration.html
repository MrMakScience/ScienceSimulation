Principles

1. Aufbau Principle

Lower energy levels must be filled completely before moving to higher ones.

1s < 2s < 2p < 3s < 3p < 4s < 3d < 4p
2. Pauli Exclusion

An orbital can hold a maximum of 2 electrons with opposite spins.

3. Hund's Rule

Electrons occupy empty orbitals of the same energy singly with parallel spins first.

Stability Exceptions

Cr (Z=24) and Cu (Z=29) promote a 4s electron to 3d to achieve stable subshells.
