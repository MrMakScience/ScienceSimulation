Isotopic mass is measured relative to Carbon-12. If an isotope is twice as heavy as Carbon-12, its ratio is 2, and its relative mass is 2 × 12 = 24.

Relative Atomic Mass is the weighted average of the relative isotopic mass of all natural isotopes on the 12C = 12.00 scale. It takes into account both the relative mass and the abundance of each isotope.
