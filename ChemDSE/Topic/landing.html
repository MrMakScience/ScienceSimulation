Visualization of Abstract Concepts 抽象概念可視化

Chemistry involves many subatomic particles that students cannot physically see or touch, making it difficult to conceptualize. This platform visualizes these invisible structures and uses intuitive, real-world metaphors to help students grasp the core concepts behind them. 化學涉及許多無法親眼看見或觸摸的亞原子微粒，這使學生難以將知識概念化。本平台將這些隱形的結構呈現出來，並運用直觀且貼近生活的比喻，幫助學生掌握其背後的化學核心概念。

magnet and clip analogy
🔄

Interactive Learning Environment 互動式學習環境

Students can directly manipulate variables to see real-time correlations between different chemical properties. The platform also features virtual experiments, allowing students to conduct lab work safely from any devices instead of relying solely on a physical laboratory. 學生可以直接操作變量，以即時觀察不同化學性質之間的關聯。平台還配備了虛擬實驗，讓學生而無需在實驗室的環境下也能在任何電子設備上安全地進行實驗操作。

migration of ions
🎮

Gamifying Rote Learning 將死記硬背融入遊戲化學習

While memorizing things like ion tables and oxidation states in redox reactions is unavoidable in chemistry, staring at a textbook isn't the only way to learn them. We have carefully designed games that turn repetitive memorization into an engaging, purposeful experience. 雖然在化學中有不少死記硬背的知識，例如離子表和主要氧化還原反應，但只看著教科書背誦並非唯一的學習途徑。我們精心設計了多款遊戲，將枯燥的重複性記憶轉化為引人入勝、具有明確目標的學習體驗。

chemical formula match game
🪜

Step-by-Step Guidance 循序漸進的引導

Complex procedures—like drawing electron diagrams or balancing redox equations—traditionally require a teacher's close supervision. Without it, students reading a textbook can easily lose themselves in the sea of text and get stuck. Our simulations solve this by providing guided, step-by-step walkthroughs that clearly show students exactly where they are and what to do next. 在傳統教學中，繪製電子結構圖或配平氧化還原方程式等複雜步驟往往需要教師的密切指導。若然學生自行閱讀教科書，他們很容易迷失在字海中而停滯不前。我們的模擬系統通過提供循序漸進的引導，清楚顯示學生當前所處的步驟以及下一步的操作，完美解決了這一難題。
