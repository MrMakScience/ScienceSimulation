VSEPR Quick Facts
  • Lone pairs exert more repulsive force than bonding pairs.
  • Repulsion order: Lone Pair-Lone Pair > Lone Pair-Bonding Pair > Bonding Pair-Bonding Pair.
  • Lone pairs demand larger spatial angles, pushing bonds closer together.
